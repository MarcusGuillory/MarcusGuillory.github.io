Data Analytics

During my MBA journey, I discovered a profound interest in data-driven courses.
Reflecting on my past experiences, it became clear that my affinity for data analysis
was a natural progression, given my previous roles and undergraduate studies where
I frequently applied similar techniques. This realization inspired me to further specialize
by pursuing a Master of Science in Information Technology Management alongside my MBA.
Below, you'll find a showcase of the projects I've completed, demonstrating my skills
and dedication to the field of Data & Analytics.

This was the final Python project for the OOP (object-oriented programming) in Python class. In this project our group was provided a data file about an apparel company consisting of 10,000 transactions. We were tasked with creating a python program to extract & clean the data, create 7 visualizations to draw business insignts from, and tell a story to the audience explaining our data and its trends as if we were presenting to an executive team seeking a business recommendation.
